problem #1: Leslie is on her way to work, and she reeeeally wants an almond milk latte. She’s also doesn’t have a whole lot of 5min extra time, so she makes a plan: she’ll go by her favorite coffee shop, Joe’s, and if they’re too busy, then she’ll swing by her second favorite spot, Mighty Bean. If Mighty bean is too busy, then she’ll just have to suck it up and drink some coffee at the office.

summary: to make an almond latter, but no extra time, then go

text

  IF leslie has extra time, THEN she makes almond milk latte herself
  IF lesle does not have 5 min extra time, THEN she goes to joe's for a coffee
  ELSE IF joe's is busy, she goes to mighty bean for a coffee
  ELSE mighty bean is busy, she goes to work and drinks a coffee there

pseudocode

  STORE beverage AS "almond milk coffee";
  STORE coffeePlace AS "house" OR "joe's" OR "mighty beans" OR "office";
  STORE extraTime AS number;

  START

  IF extraTime >= 5min
    OUTPUT make beverage in the "house";
  ELSE  
    IF "joe's" is not too busy
      OUTPUT get beverage from the "joe's";
    ELSE  
      IF "mighty beans" is not too busy
        OUTPUT get beverage from the "mighty beans";
      ELSE
        OUTPUT make beverage at the "office"

  STOP
